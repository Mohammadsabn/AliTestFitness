PROFESSIONAL INSTALLATION & RELOCATION

Ensure the longevity of your new equipment and the safe, damage-free transfer of existing machines.

OUR EXPERTISE

New Equipment Installation

The correct initial setup is crucial for avoiding future breakdowns. We handle the entire assembly process for all commercial brands.

  • Precision Assembly: Calibration of moving parts (belts, decks, cables).
  • Load Testing: Thorough testing of machines under load conditions.
  • Full Configuration: Setting up electronic consoles and network connections.

Gym Relocation & Shifting

We manage the complex logistics of moving heavy fitness equipment, from disassembly to final setup at the new location.

  • Safe Disassembly: Careful dismantling and packaging of all components.
  • Transport Guidance: Ensuring equipment is correctly secured for transit.
  • Reassembly & Calibration: Installation and testing at the final site.

THE RELOCATION ROADMAP

1. Site Inspection

Assessment of current and new locations for planning and logistics.

2. Careful Disassembly

Machines are professionally broken down and labeled to prevent damage.

3. Secure Packaging

Components are securely packed for transport (Note: Transportation is client responsibility).

4. Reassembly & Test

Reinstallation, full testing, and handover at the new facility.

*Note: Ali Fitness Services handles the dismantling and reassembly of the equipment. Transportation logistics are typically managed by the client or a third-party mover, ensuring flexibility.
